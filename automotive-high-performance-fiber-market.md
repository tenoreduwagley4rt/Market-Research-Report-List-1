Polybenzimidazole (PBI) delivers high thermal resistance, and polyphenylene sulfide (PPS) offers chemical resistance and dimensional stability. Glass fiber is known for its affordability and adequate strength, while high-strength polyethylene is lightweight and resistant to moisture. Other materials include advanced composites and specialty fibers, catering to diverse automotive applications.</p></p>
