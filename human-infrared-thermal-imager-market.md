<p><p>The Human Infrared Thermal Imager market encompasses two primary types: photon detection and thermal detection. Photon detection utilizes high-sensitivity sensors to identify infrared radiation emitted directly by human bodies, offering precise measurements for various applications, including security and healthcare. In contrast, thermal detection measures the thermal radiation emitted by objects, providing a broader view of heat signatures in the environment. Both technologies are essential for advancements in surveillance, search and rescue operations, and medical diagnostics, enhancing human safety and efficiency.</p></p>
