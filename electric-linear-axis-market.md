<p><p>The electric linear axis market comprises various types tailored to specific applications. Spindle drive electric linear axes leverage a screw mechanism, providing high precision and load capacity, ideal for demanding applications. Driveless linear guide units offer a flexible solution where external drives are used, allowing for modular designs and space-saving configurations. Each type addresses unique operational requirements in automation and motion control.</p></p>
