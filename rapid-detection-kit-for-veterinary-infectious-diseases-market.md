<p><p>The Rapid Detection Kit for Veterinary Infectious Diseases market is divided into two main types: Antibody Detection and Antigen Detection. Antibody Detection kits identify immune responses to pathogens, indicating previous exposure to infections in animals. In contrast, Antigen Detection kits target specific proteins from the pathogens, allowing for the immediate identification of active infections. Both types are essential for timely diagnosis, enabling effective management of infectious diseases in veterinary practices, thus enhancing animal health and welfare.</p></p>
