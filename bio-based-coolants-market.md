<p><p>The bio-based coolants market comprises two primary types: semi-synthetic and synthetic bio-based coolants. Semi-synthetic bio-based coolants combine traditional mineral oils with bio-based ingredients, offering enhanced performance and reduced environmental impact. These coolants often provide excellent lubrication and thermal management. In contrast, synthetic bio-based coolants are entirely formulated from renewable resources, delivering superior thermal stability and biodegradability. They are designed for high-performance applications and cater to sustainability-conscious industries, reducing reliance on fossil fuels and minimizing ecological footprints.</p></p>
