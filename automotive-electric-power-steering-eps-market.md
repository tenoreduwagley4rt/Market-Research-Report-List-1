<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Automotive Electric Power Steering (EPS) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>C-EPS</li><li>P-EPS</li><li>R-EPS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive Electric Power Steering (EPS) systems enhance steering control by using electric motors instead of traditional hydraulic systems, leading to improved efficiency and reduced weight. The market is segmented into three types: C-EPS (Column Electric Power Steering) integrates the motor within the steering column, providing a compact solution. P-EPS (Pinion Electric Power Steering) places the motor on the gear mechanism, offering direct torque assistance. R-EPS (Rack Electric Power Steering) combines the motor with the steering rack, delivering precise handling and responsiveness.</p></p>
