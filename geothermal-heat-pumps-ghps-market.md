<p><p>Geothermal Heat Pumps (GHPs) harness the earth's stable temperature for heating and cooling. The market consists of three main types: </p><p>1. **Vertical Closed Loop**: Utilizes deep vertical boreholes for heat exchange, ideal where land space is limited.</p><p>   </p><p>2. **Horizontal Closed Loop**: Involves shallow horizontal trenches, accommodating larger spaces and being cost-effective.</p><p>3. **Open Loop**: Draws groundwater directly for heat exchange, efficient but reliant on suitable water sources and regulatory considerations. Each type caters to varying site conditions and energy needs.</p></p>
