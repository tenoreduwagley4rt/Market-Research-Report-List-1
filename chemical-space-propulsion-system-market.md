<p><p>The chemical space propulsion system market is divided into four main types: </p><p>1. **Solid Propellant** systems utilize a solid fuel and oxidizer mixture, offering high thrust and simplicity. </p><p>2. **Liquid Propellant** systems use separate liquid fuels and oxidizers, providing better control and efficiency. </p><p>3. **Hybrid Propellant** combines solid and liquid components, balancing performance and safety. </p><p>4. **Cold Gas** systems use inert gases, ideal for low-thrust applications and maneuvering, often favored in small satellites and spacecraft. Each type serves distinct mission profiles and requirements.</p></p>
